Leading design for the Crypto.com App

Crypto.com operates with a centralized model where Product Design Managers report to the Director of Product Design. Each Product Design Manager is responsible for a core product in the Crypto.com ecosystem (Crypto.com App, Exchange, and DeFi Wallet). Before I transitioned to management, I led the 0-1 design for Tectonic, a borrowing & lending DeFi protocol on the Cronos chain, and the core trading, onboarding, and card experience on the Crypto.com App.

As the Product Design Manager of the Crypto.com App, I built and led a team of designers and researchers working remotely from London, Stockholm, Singapore, and Hong Kong.

Creating the vision for the Crypto.com app

In 2022, I led an initiative to create a vision for the Crypto.com app. We talked to our users, ran workshops, and analyzed their behavior data to understand their journey into the crypto space.

We created a vision where the experience focused on the core jobs to be done for our users:

  1. Discover
  2. Trade
  3. Manage portfolio and make use of assets

Safe and inspiring onboarding experience

We designed an onboarding experience that felt safe and inspiring to newcomers. We surfaced information about their local crypto regulations, trading risks, and how they could cash out after buying crypto.

Onboarding experience: 27% higher conversion in KYC submission

Onboarding experience: 24% increase in first crypto purchase conversion, 55% increase in trading volume per user

Educational hub x Rewards program: 8x ROI, 1.4x DAU

Easy access to market insights and opportunities

For retail traders with a solid understanding of finance, we provided easy access to insights and market opportunities to help them make trading decisions.

A simple trading experience for beginners and advanced traders

As our users became more experienced, we added more tools (order types, derivatives) to help them trade while continuing to make the overall experience more convenient. We experimented with ideas like simplifying the market order flow, quick inputs, tutorial animations, and intuitive UI to make complex financial products easy to understand.

In one of our experiments, we displayed simple animations to help new users understand how they can benefit from different order types. This resulted in a 9% uplift in spot trading conversion.
