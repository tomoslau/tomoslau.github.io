Hi, I’m a designer living in Vancouver.

Most recently, I was a Product Design Manager at Crypto.com where I led a team of designers and researchers to build the safest and most convenient crypto exchange, and designed the v1 of Tectonic, a defi lending protocol on Cronos. Previously, I was a designer at Wristcheck, Foodora & Foodpanda, GogoX, and Hypebeast.

I was born and raised in Hong Kong, and studied Advertising at Savannah College of Art and Design. Prior to becoming a product designer, I was a film production assistant, and an Art Director at Dentsu Isobar, where I worked with brands like Audi, Disney, and Estee Lauder.
